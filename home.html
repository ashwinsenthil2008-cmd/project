TAMILNADU

Tamil Nadu is a beautiful state in the southern part of India. It is famous for its rich culture, temples, and traditional dance like Bharatanatyam.

KERALA

Kerala is a beautiful state located in the southwestern part of India. It is known as “God Own Country” because of its scenic beauty, backwaters, and lush green landscapes.

MYSORE

Mysore Palace is one of the most famous historical monuments in India, located in Mysuru, Karnataka. It was the royal residence and is known for its beautiful Indo-Saracenic architecture.

NEW DELHI

The Taj Mahal is a world-famous monument located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Made of white marble, the Taj Mahal is known for its beautiful symmetrical gardens.

MUMBAI

Mumbai is the capital city of Maharashtra and one of the largest cities in India. It is known as the financial capital of the country and is home to the Bollywood film industry.

ANDHRA PRADESH

Tirupati is a famous pilgrimage city located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is best known for the Sri Venkateswara Temple, also called Tirumala Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara.
